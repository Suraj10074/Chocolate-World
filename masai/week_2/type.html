MILK CHOCOLATE Spoil every chocolate lover in your life with decadent milk chocolate gifts. Milk chocolate is classically made from dark chocolate of low cocoa solid content and a higher sugar content plus a milk product, which may be in the form of boiled milk, milk powder or condensed milk, all of which give variations of a much admired slight caramel taste.

CHOCOLATE TRUFFLES A chocolate truffle is a type of chocolate confectionery, traditionally made with a chocolate ganache centre coated in chocolate, cocoa powder or chopped toasted nuts (typically hazelnuts, almonds, or coconut), usually in a spherical, conical, or curved shape.
